"A COMBINED USE OF THE SOFT SYSTEM METHODOLOGY AND CONSTRUCTIVIST MULTICRITERIA DECISION AID TO SUPPORT A DECISION-MAKER IN A COMPLEX SITUATION"
"ÁREA: 06. PESQUISA OPERACIONAL / 06.2. Decisão Multicriterial"
"AUTORES:"
"ANDRÉ ANDRADE LONGARAY;LEONARDO ENSSLIN;JOHN ROBERT MACKNESS"
"operations research; multi criteria decision aid; soft systems methodology"
"To decide is part of the daily one of the people. Either in the scope of the professional or particular relationships, decisions are taken all the moment. However, the increase of the degree of complexity of the situations, the excess of available information, the diversity of involved interests and the limitations human beings in the processing of simultaneous events, make with that the emphasis is come back toward the choice amongst a set of alternatives, instead of the identification of which involved objectives and as to make to improve the performance in each one of them. In this direction, boardings concerned to the process of support to the decision, as the Multi Criteria Decision Aid Constructivist (MCDA-C), represents an important instrument to assist individuals in problematic situations. One of its main characteristics is methodological flexibility, decurrent of the vision of constructivist knowledge that guides it. Inserted in this context, the present paper has as objective present the development of the proposal theoretician-methodological who makes possible the integration of Soft Systems Methodology (SSM) to the MCDA-C in the processes of the decision aid. With this, it is intended to powerful the happened benefits of the use of process SSM in the activity of representation, organization and understanding of the problem, uniting them it robustness and focus in the learning generation on the problem, characteristics that guide the MCDA-Constructivist. Using a real situation, we present the process of integration of SSM with MCDA-C by three phases. In the first structuring phase, SSM helped to scope and structure the situation and smooth the path towards a hierarchical tree structure using MCDA-C and the exploration of situation descriptors. In the evaluation phase, the values of local preferences and the compensation rates between the descriptors are determined together with local and global evaluations of the different criteria used in the model. Finally, using a comparison between different scenarios of decisor performance together with sensitivity analysis, the recommendations phase led to the development of a really useful Decision Support System for the decisor."

"A HYBRID METAHEURISTIC FOR THE VEHICLE ROUTING PROBLEM WITH SIMULTANEOUS PICK-UP AND DELIVERY"
"ÁREA: 06. PESQUISA OPERACIONAL / 06.1. Programação Matemática"
"AUTORES:"
"ANAND SUBRAMANIAN;LUCÍDIO DOS ANJOS FORMIGA CABRAL;GUSTAVO REZENDE CARVALHO"
"vehicle routing problem with simultaneous pick-up and delivery, grasp, vnd."
"This paper deals with the Vehicle Routing Problem with Simultaneous Pick-up and Delivery (VRPSPD). Due to the combinatorial nature of the problem, heuristics methods are commonly used to generate good quality solutions in an acceptable computational time. Therefore, a greedy randomized adaptive search procedure that uses a variable neighborhood descent method to perform the local search is proposed. The algorithm was tested in benchmark problems found in the literature related to the VRPSPD. The results appear to be competitive compared to the best known solutions."

"A TABU SEARCH ALGORITHM FOR THE GENERALIZED MINIMUM SPANNING TREE PROBLEM"
"ÁREA: 06. PESQUISA OPERACIONAL / 06.1. Programação Matemática"
"AUTORES:"
"FERNANDO DE CRISTO;FELIPE MULLER;LUCIANO FERREIRA;DENIS BORENSTEIN"
"generalized minimum spanning tree. graph optimization. meta-heuristics"
"The generalized minimum spanning tree problem (GMST) is present in many situations of the real world, such as in the context of telecommunications, transports and data clustering, where a network of clusters needs to be connected using one node from each cluster. In this work is presented the design and implementation of a tabu search algorithm for the GMST problem. In our computational tests on 150 TSPLIB instances generated with the Center and Grid Clustering grouping procedure, our tabu search algorithm found the optimal solution in 146 instances in a very low computational effort."

"AN APPLICATION OF MULTIPLE CORRESPONDENCE ANALYSIS TO THE CLASSIFICATION OF RESIDENTIAL ELECTRICITY CONSUMERS"
"ÁREA: 06. PESQUISA OPERACIONAL / 06.6. Análise de Demandas por Produtos"
"AUTORES:"
"RONALDO ROCHA BASTOS;HENRIQUE STEINHERZ HIPPERT;AUGUSTO CARVALHO SOUZA"
"classification; logistic regression; multiple correspondence analysis; artificial neural networks; load forecasting"
"Classification of consumers from relatively small areas into different demand groups may help in the estimation of where, and by how much, electricity consumption is likely to change, thus reducing the uncertainties in the aggregate projections required from regional electricity supply companies in Brazil by the Câmara de Comercialização de Energia Elétrica (Electricity Commercialisation Chamber), responsible for the purchase of all energy generated nation-wide, since 2004.  In this paper we compare some methods of classification which can account for the socio-economic profile of electricity consumers and the characteristics of households that generate such residential consumption.  In particular, we were interested in studying the usefulness of Multiple Correspondence Analysis (MCA), a multivariate exploratory technique for categorical data, in the identification of covariates and in the combination of its results with regression modelling and intensive computation techniques. The strategies analysed were logistic regression (LR) with both the original categorical covariates and with MCA factors as covariates, and artificial neural networks (ANN) with both types of input variables.  The data used in this paper come from a multiple-stage sample survey undertaken in 2004-2005 among the residential electricity consumers of the city of Juiz de Fora, state of Minas Gerais, an area supplied by a single regional electricity company.  The results obtained have shown the importance of MCA as an intermediate step to estimate coefficients that can be used in classification.  For LR, despite the similar performance of both strategies, a significant reduction of the dimensionality of the problem was attained.  As to the ANN, a methodology originally designed for numerical predictors, the use of MCA factors not only improves the performance of the method, but permits its application to categorical predictors."

"FLÁVIO MOLINA DA SILVA;SILVIO ALEXANDRE DE ARAUJO"
"dimensionamento de lotes, custo de transporte, restrições de capacidade, otimização inteira"
"De acordo com Norden e Velde (2005), embora os custos de transporte representem mais de 50% do custo logístico total de um produto, este é praticamente negligenciado na modelagem dos problemas de dimensionamento de lotes. Dentre os poucos estudos de dimensionamento de lotes que consideram custos de transporte, a maioria tem em comum a suposição de que a taxa para o transporte diminui de acordo com o aumento do volume transportado.O trabalho de Norden e Velde (2005) considera uma empresa que, ao produzir seus produtos finais, tem que transportá-los até um armazém e, além disso, o transporte é terceirizado. Para isso, é feito um contrato com uma empresa que estabelece os seguintes custos para o transporte: um custo fixo de contrato, um custo, por pálete, para um determinado número de páletes transportados e um custo adicional para o transporte dos páletes extras. Neste caso, a alta produção dos itens em apenas um período pode encarecer o transporte, criando um trade-off entre o custo de transporte e o planejamento da produção."
"No presente trabalho, tem-se uma extensão do trabalho de Norden e Velde (2005) que consiste em considerar restrições de capacidade de máquina e a possibilidade de atraso no atendimento à demanda. Será feita uma análise quanto a dificuldade de resolução do modelo com restrições de capacidade, através de um pacote de otimização inteira-mista. Além disso, pretende-se avaliar os métodos utilizados pelo pacote de otimização inteira AMPL/Cplex 10.0."

"CONSIDERAÇÃO DE SERVIDORES HETEROGÊNEOS NA GENERALIZAÇÃO DE FILAS M/M/C"
"ÁREA: 06. PESQUISA OPERACIONAL / 06.3. Processos Estocásticos"
"AUTORES:"
"FREDERICO SAMARTINI QUEIROZ ALVES;LUIS ANTONIO CAPANEMA PEDROSA;HANI CAMILLE YEHIA"
"teoria de filas, servidores heterogêneos, medidas de desempenho.      -    queueing theory,  heterogeneous servers,  performance measures."
"RESUMO"
"Uma generalização para o sistema de fila M/M/c é proposta. Os c servidores foram considerados como sendo heterogêneos, o que resultou em uma expansão do espaço de estados. Considerando apenas as possibilidades de maior probabilidade foi oportuno fazer uma redução desse espaço de estados e desenvolver analiticamente uma equação para se obter o pior caso não só para o tempo médio de espera na fila e para o número médio de pessoas na fila, como também para o tempo médio no sistema e o número médio de pessoas no sistema. Essa formulação se torna atraente por ser capaz de aproximar o comportamento real desses sistemas de servidores heterogêneos com um erro menor do que se fosse feito por uma MMc tradicional, além de ser um limite superior para as medidas de performance desses.  A validação do esquema proposto é feita por comparações com simulações em GPSS."
"ABSTRACT"
"A generalization for a M/M/c queueing system is proposed. The c servers were considered heterogeneous what resulted in an expanded state space.
